About me

The ghost behind
the machine.

I'm TRABY-CASPER — a self-taught developer and founder based in Nairobi, Kenya. I started building things on the internet out of curiosity and never stopped. What began as a passion for problem-solving grew into two companies and a stack of production services running 24/7.

I specialise in backend infrastructure, Linux server management, and building products that actually work at scale. Whether it's spinning up a game server panel, writing a custom API, or wiring up an automated billing system — I care about reliability, clean code, and shipping things that last.

As CEO of XCASPER Hosting and the engineering lead at Casper Tech Devs, I handle everything from architecture decisions to late-night debugging sessions. That's just how solo founders roll.

My Journey

How I got here.

Every project, every late night, every deploy — this is the story so far.

💡
2021
Wrote my first lines of code
Discovered programming through web development. Started with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — built small projects and fell in love with the idea of creating things from nothing.
⚙️
2022
Backend & Linux deep-dive
Moved into PHP, Node.js, and Linux server management. Rented my first VPS, broke it several times, learned how it all works under the hood. Became obsessed with DevOps and uptime.
🚀
2023
Founded Casper Tech Devs
Launched Casper Tech Devs to offer API development and technical services. Shipped the Movie API, the Pairing API, and the RC Solver — all running in production on a managed PM2 stack.
🖥️
2024
Launched XCASPER Hosting
Built XCASPER Hosting from scratch — custom Pterodactyl panel, Paystack KES billing, a support system, status page, and automated installer. Went from zero to a live, SSL-secured, multi-service hosting platform.
🌍
Now
Building globally, rooted in Kenya
Running two companies, managing infrastructure across multiple domains, and continuing to ship. Always looking for interesting technical problems to solve and people to build with.
